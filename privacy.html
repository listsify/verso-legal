Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Verso ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered daily planning application that helps users organize tasks, track habits, set goals, and plan their day through voice and text input, with the support of an AI assistant ("Kai"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the Verso mobile application (the "App" or "Service").

By downloading, accessing, or using Verso, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Who We Are

Verso is operated by Adam Mentel, an individual developer based in Poland. For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, you can reach us at hello@verso.website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Name / Username — collected during account registration
  • Email address — collected during account registration and used for authentication
  • Password — collected during registration; passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • Job title — collected during onboarding to personalize your experience
  • User-generated content — the tasks, journal entries, goals, habits, and notes you create within the App
  • Voice recordings / audio data — collected when you use voice input to create tasks or plan your day; processed for speech-to-text conversion

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Log and usage data — service-related, diagnostic, and performance information, including how you interact with the App's features
  • Device data — information about your device such as device model, operating system version, and application identifiers

We do not collect your precise geolocation. Verso does not use cookies, as it is a mobile application rather than a website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver and facilitate the core functionality of the App (planning, tasks, habits, goals, journaling)
  • Power AI-driven features, such as voice-to-task parsing and personalized assistant responses from Kai, in order to provide the App's core functionality
  • Personalize your experience based on your preferences and usage patterns
  • Identify usage trends, in order to analyze how the Service is used so we can improve it
  • Protect the Service, in order to diagnose problems and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send administrative information, such as updates to this Policy or important notices about your account
  • Request feedback to understand how you use the App and improve your experience
  • Process subscription payments for Verso Pro

We do not use your information for targeted advertising, and we do not send marketing or promotional communications.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA / UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process your personal information under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a Contract — to provide the Services you have signed up for
  • Consent — where you have given explicit permission, such as for microphone access
  • Legitimate Interests — to analyze usage trends and improve the App, in a manner that does not override your rights
  • Legal Obligation — where we are required to retain or disclose information to comply with the law
  • Vital Interests — in rare circumstances where processing is necessary to protect someone's life

6. Payments

Verso offers a free tier and a paid subscription tier ("Verso Pro"). All payments are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system and managed via RevenueCat. Verso does not collect, process, or store any credit card, debit card, or billing address information. Apple's privacy practices for payments are described at apple.com/legal/privacy, and RevenueCat's at revenuecat.com/privacy.

7. AI-Powered Features

Verso uses Anthropic's Claude API to power features such as voice-to-task parsing, personalized task and goal suggestions, and the Kai assistant. When you use these features, relevant content (such as voice transcripts or task descriptions) is sent to Anthropic for processing. We do not use your personal information to train AI models.

You may opt out of AI-based processing at any time by not using voice input or assistant features, by deleting your account in Settings, or by contacting us at hello@verso.website.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active, in order to provide you with the Service. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data from our active systems. Some information may be retained for a limited period where necessary to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

9. How We Protect Your Information

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted password storage, and database-level access controls (Row Level Security) through our infrastructure provider, Supabase. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Data Transfers

Verso is operated from Poland. Your information may be processed by our third-party service providers in countries outside of your own, including the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses to protect data transferred internationally.

If you are located in the EEA or UK, you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to certain processing, subject to applicable law.

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion of your information, the right to equal service regardless of whether you exercise your privacy rights, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information.

We do not sell your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@verso.website. We will respond to verifiable requests within the timeframe required by applicable law.

12. Children's Privacy

Verso is not directed at children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@verso.website and we will take steps to delete it.
